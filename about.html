About us

Jostas was founded by a former graffiti artist turned professional painter who grew up in the city streets, watching vibrant murals transform dull walls into landmarks. Frustrated by big painting companies that only cared about neat suburban homes, he built a crew of skilled artisans who understood the soul of urban spaces — parks, skate parks, community centres, sports courts, warehouses, and public buildings. The name "Jostas" comes from the old neighbourhood slang for "those who show up" — because no job is too rough, too big, or too creative. Every stroke they lay down is a statement that public and recreational spaces deserve to look extraordinary.
